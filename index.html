My name is Cynthia Weaver.

I am a former English Language Arts teacher who recently made the move from Lakeland, Florida to Asheville, North Carolina. I have a Bachelor of Arts in Music and English from The University of Alabama, and I am working towards a Master of Science degree in Learning Design and Technology in an online program at the University of South Florida. Currently, I am studying software development at Thinkful.

When I'm not busy coding, I enjoy exploring the great outdoors through hiking and backpacking. Living in Asheville, I am fortunate to have access to some of the most beautiful trails and scenery in the United States, and I make it a priority to get out and enjoy them as often as possible.
